# Creative Brief — Face Detect + Split Podcast Update

## Goal
Announce a 2-in-1 product update in a single IG post:
1. **Smarter face detection** — face tracking now locks the speaker tighter, no more cut-off heads on 9:16/1:1.
2. **Split-screen podcast layout** — stack 2 speakers top/bottom in one vertical clip, perfect for podcast Reels/TikToks.

Hook angle: podcast clippers no longer have to choose between "show host" or "show guest" — they get both, framed perfectly.

## Audience
- Primary: podcasters and interview-style YouTubers repurposing long episodes into shorts.
- Secondary: agency/social media managers cutting client podcasts.

## Format
- **Layout**: Center spotlight (vertical phone mockup at center, brand frame around).
- **Canvas**: 1080×1080 (square IG post).
- **Composition rule**: max 3 brand colors. Slight ±5° rotation on stickers.

## Visual Concept
Center of frame: a chunky illustrated phone (vertical, 9:16 screen), comic-outlined. The phone screen shows a **split podcast clip**:
- Top half: cartoon podcaster A (mic in hand, headphones, mid-laugh) with a small dashed face-tracking square locked on the face.
- Bottom half: cartoon podcaster B (different look, mic, smiling) with the same dashed tracking square on the face.
- Thick zigzag/torn divider line between top and bottom halves.
- Karaoke caption bar at the very bottom of the phone screen ("THIS IS WILD" in white pill).

Around the phone:
- Top-left corner: yellow starburst badge with "NEW!" in deep navy bold caps.
- Top-right: small "SellOn" wordmark with flame icon.
- Bottom-left of phone: orange chunky arrow pointing to the dashed face-tracking square + small label "SMART FACE LOCK".
- Bottom-right of phone: purple chunky arrow pointing to the divider + small label "SPLIT PODCAST".
- Background: warm off-white `#FFF9F0` with subtle halftone dots and 2-3 motion lines radiating from the phone.
- Bottom strip: "Slap. Clip. Boom." in deep navy, small.
