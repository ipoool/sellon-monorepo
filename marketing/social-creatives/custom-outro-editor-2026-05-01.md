# Creative Brief — Custom Outro Editor

## Goal
Announce + upsell the Custom Outro Editor — a Pro/Mega exclusive feature that lets users brand every clip with their own avatar, username, social handles, and colors. Distinct from the default outro (SellOn animation, all users): this is YOUR brand, YOUR face, YOUR clip ending.

Hook angle: Free users end every clip with SellOn's brand. Pro users end every clip with theirs.

## Audience
- Primary: creators already using SellOn on free/Starter who haven't upgraded
- Secondary: new creators deciding between tiers

## Format
- **Layout**: Side-by-side comparison — two phone screens, left = FREE default outro, right = PRO custom outro
- **Canvas**: 1080×1080 (square IG post)
- **Composition rule**: max 3 brand colors, hard shadows, ±5° rotations

## Visual Concept

**Background**: warm off-white `#FFF9F0`, halftone dot pattern subtle 15% opacity.

**Left phone** (slightly tilted -5°, smaller):
- Screen shows a simple outro frame: "SellOn" wordmark + flame icon, centered, on orange background
- Label below: "FREE" in a deep navy rounded pill badge
- A subtle "X" or lock emoji vibe — not literally crossed out, but presented as lesser

**Right phone** (slightly tilted +4°, bigger and taller — hero element):
- Screen shows a custom outro frame with:
  - Circular avatar photo placeholder (cartoon illustrated face with headphones — expressive, comic style)
  - Username "@yournamehere" in bold below avatar
  - 3 small social icons (IG / TikTok / YouTube) row below username
  - Background: user's custom color — shown as purple #6C5CE7
  - Bottom bar: "Follow for more" in a white caption pill
- Label below: "PRO" in a yellow #FFE156 starburst badge, bold deep navy text, slightly rotated

**Between the phones** (center overlap area):
- Chunky orange arrow pointing RIGHT (from left phone → right phone), bold outline

**Top of canvas**:
- Headline text: "Your outro. Your brand." — large, Fredoka bold, deep navy, slight left-alignment
- Optional small "✦ Pro & Mega" tag in purple pill, top-right

**Bottom strip**:
- "Slap. Clip. Boom." in deep navy small centered text

## Color Plan (3 max)
- **Orange `#FF8A50`** — arrow, accent, FREE phone screen tint
- **Purple `#6C5CE7`** — PRO phone screen background, PRO pill
- **Yellow `#FFE156`** — PRO badge/starburst
- Outlines + text: Deep Navy `#1A1A2E`
- Background: Off-white `#FFF9F0`
