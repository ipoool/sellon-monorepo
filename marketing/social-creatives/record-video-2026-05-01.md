# Creative Brief — Record Video: Record, Trim, Clip

## Goal
Showcase the Record Video feature — browser-based recording that combines screen share + circular camera PiP, built-in auto-scroll teleprompter, and a trim view before uploading. Zero external tools needed. Hook angle: your whole recording + editing studio is already in your browser.

## Audience
- Educators, coaches, tutorial creators, online course sellers
- Creators who want to record talking-head content but hate setting up OBS or external tools
- Anyone who's nervous on camera and needs a teleprompter

## Format
- **Layout**: Laptop screen mockup center-stage showing the recording interface
- **Canvas**: 1080×1080
- **Composition rule**: max 3 brand colors, dynamic tilt, hard shadows

## Visual Concept

**Background**: warm off-white `#FFF9F0`, subtle halftone dots 15% opacity, 2–3 speed lines radiating from the laptop.

**CENTER — Laptop Mockup** (slightly tilted -3°, large, fills most of canvas):
- Chunky illustrated laptop, thick deep navy outline stroke 4px, orange `#FF8A50` screen bezel highlight, hard offset drop shadow 8px no blur `#1A1A2E`
- **Laptop screen contents** (the recording UI, flat 2D illustration style):
  - Main screen area: dark background showing a "screen share" placeholder — simple illustrated desktop/browser window, flat comic style
  - Bottom-left of screen: circular camera feed "PiP" — a cartoon illustrated face (expressive, big eyes, mid-speech with open mouth) inside a white-outlined circle with deep navy border, slightly lifted with a small drop shadow
  - Top-left of screen: a red circle "REC" indicator badge glowing — red circle + "REC" text in white bold small caps
  - Center-bottom overlay: teleprompter text strip — semi-transparent dark pill containing scrolling text "...and that's why you should repurpose your content every single day..." in white rounded font. A small arrow "▼" at the right edge indicating auto-scroll
  - Top-right of screen: a small orange "CLIP IT" button placeholder

**TOP of canvas**:
- Headline: "Record. Trim. Clip." — large Fredoka bold, deep navy, centered. Each word punchy, minimal

**Around the laptop** (floating labels with chunky arrows):
- Left side: orange arrow pointing to the camera circle PiP → small label tag "CAMERA PiP" in white bold on orange rounded rect
- Right side: purple `#6C5CE7` arrow pointing to the teleprompter strip → small label tag "TELEPROMPTER" in white bold on purple rounded rect

**BOTTOM of canvas**:
- Small yellow `#FFE156` starburst badge: "FREE TO USE" in deep navy bold caps, slightly rotated
- "Slap. Clip. Boom." deep navy small centered

**Brand mark**: "SellOn" + flame icon, top-right, small, deep navy.
