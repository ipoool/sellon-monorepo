# Creative Brief — Slideshow: Stack Photos → Instant Video

## Goal
Announce the new Slideshow source type. Users can now stack a pile of images → SellOn turns them into a 9:16 TikTok/Reels-ready video with per-slide text automatically. Hook angle: no video needed — photos are enough.

## Audience
- Lifestyle creators, photographers, brands with product photos
- Creators who feel "stuck" because they only have photos, not video footage
- Small business owners wanting social content from product shots

## Format
- **Layout**: Transformation concept — pile of photos on left → arrow → phone mockup on right
- **Canvas**: 1080×1080
- **Composition rule**: max 3 brand colors, dynamic rotation, hard shadows

## Visual Concept

**Background**: warm off-white `#FFF9F0`, subtle halftone dot texture 15% opacity.

**LEFT SIDE — Photo Stack**:
A playful pile of 4–5 illustrated polaroid-style photo cards, each slightly rotated (±5–12°), stacked on top of each other with hard offset drop shadows. Each card is a white rectangle with a thick deep navy outline. The top card shows a simplified comic illustration inside (a mountain landscape, flat 2D style). The other cards peek out from behind. The whole stack sits tilted, giving a "scattered pile" vibe.
- Above the stack: small orange starburst label "JUST PHOTOS" in deep navy bold caps

**CENTER — Big Arrow**:
A large chunky orange `#FF8A50` arrow pointing RIGHT, thick deep navy outline stroke 3px, bold comic filled style. Slightly tilted upward.

**RIGHT SIDE — Phone Mockup with 9:16 Video**:
A vertical smartphone (9:16 proportions), chunky comic outline, purple `#6C5CE7` frame highlight, hard offset shadow 6px no blur. Phone screen shows:
- A TikTok-style vertical video playing — the first "slide" shows the same mountain illustration filling the screen
- A white text overlay bar at the bottom: "Summer 2024 🏔️" in bold rounded font inside a rounded rectangle pill
- A small purple progress bar at the bottom of the screen (slide 1 of 5)
- TikTok-style UI hint: small ❤️ and 💬 icons on the right edge

**TOP of canvas**:
- Headline: "No video? No problem." — large Fredoka bold, deep navy, centered or left-aligned
- Small "NEW ✦" yellow starburst badge top-right, rotated +6°

**BOTTOM of canvas**:
- "Slap. Clip. Boom." deep navy small centered, Fredoka bold

**Extra detail**: 2–3 motion speed lines radiating from the arrow area for kinetic energy.

## Color Plan (3 max)
- **Orange `#FF8A50`** — arrow, accent, "JUST PHOTOS" label
- **Purple `#6C5CE7`** — phone frame, video progress bar
- **Yellow `#FFE156`** — NEW starburst badge
- Outlines + text: Deep Navy `#1A1A2E`
- Background: Off-white `#FFF9F0`
