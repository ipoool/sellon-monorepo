# Creative Brief — Affiliate QR in Clips

## Goal
Announce the Affiliate QR feature. Creators can now burn a QR code linked to any product URL directly into their clips — it shows for up to 10 seconds, survives any platform (TikTok/IG/YT strip clickable links but can't strip pixels), and every scan is tracked with source breakdown (QR scan / page CTA / external). Open to all logged-in users.

Hook angle: platforms strip your affiliate links — but they can't strip pixels. Your QR is in the video. It always gets through.

## Audience
- Product reviewers, unboxers, affiliate marketers on TikTok/IG/YouTube
- Creators doing brand collabs who need trackable links in short-form video
- Dropshippers and small brand owners pushing products via clips

## Format
- **Layout**: Center hero — phone mockup with QR visible on screen, analytics floating badge
- **Canvas**: 1080×1080
- **Composition rule**: max 3 brand colors, dynamic tilts, hard shadows

## Visual Concept

**Background**: warm off-white `#FFF9F0`, halftone dot texture 15% opacity.

**HEADLINE** (top, large, centered):
- Line 1: **"They can't strip pixels."** — very large Fredoka bold, deep navy `#1A1A2E`
- Line 2 below, smaller: *"Burn your affiliate link into every clip."* — deep navy, regular weight

**CENTER HERO — Vertical Phone Mockup** (slightly tilted +3°):
- Large vertical smartphone (9:16), purple `#6C5CE7` frame highlight, thick deep navy outline stroke 4px, hard offset shadow 7px no blur `#1A1A2E`
- **Phone screen contents** (flat 2D illustration):
  - Main video frame: a cartoon product reviewer character — comic style, flat 2D, expressive face, holding a product box in one hand, pointing at it with the other. Orange shirt, bold outlines. Background of screen: a simple room wall, flat color.
  - **Bottom-right corner of phone screen**: a white rounded-rectangle box (QR overlay) containing a simple black QR code pattern (flat illustrated, bold squares, not realistic). Below the QR: tiny text "Scan to shop →" in deep navy very small
  - **Bottom of screen**: a white karaoke caption bar pill: "this product is WILD 🔥" bold text

**FLOATING ELEMENTS around the phone:**
