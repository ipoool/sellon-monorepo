# Creative Brief — Pricing vs Competitor

## Goal
Drive sign-ups dengan kontras harga yang tidak bisa diabaikan. Angle: "lebih murah dari makan siang kamu." Kompetitor charging langganan bulanan Rp 200.000+. SellOn mulai Rp 25.000 sekali bayar, kredit tidak expired, bayar pakai QRIS/GoPay/OVO/DANA/ShopeePay — tidak perlu kartu USD.

Actual pricing (from /pricing page):
- Starter: Rp 25.000 → 10 clips (Rp 2.500/clip)
- Pro: Rp 100.000 → 50 clips (Rp 2.000/clip) — Most Popular
- Mega: Rp 300.000 → 200 clips (Rp 1.500/clip)

## Audience
- Kreator Indonesia yang sudah dengar soal tools AI clipper tapi mundur karena harga atau butuh kartu USD
- Pengguna free/trial yang belum upgrade karena takut mahal
- Kreator yang bayar langganan tool lain dan tidak worth it

## Format
- **Layout**: Split comparison card — two price tags, left vs right
- **Canvas**: 1080×1080
- **Composition rule**: max 3 brand colors, hard shadows, bold contrast between two sides

## Visual Concept

**Background**: warm off-white `#FFF9F0`, halftone dot texture 15% opacity.

**HEADLINE** (top, centered, large):
Text: **"Cheaper Than Your Lunch."** — large Fredoka bold, deep navy `#1A1A2E`.
Subline below in smaller text: *"Mulai Rp 25.000. Bayar sekali, kredit nggak expired."* — deep navy, lighter weight.

**CENTER MAIN ELEMENT — Two price tag cards side by side:**

**LEFT CARD — "Langganan Bulanan" tag** (slightly tilted -4°, smaller):
- Shape: a classic hanging price tag shape (rectangle with rounded top corners + small hole + string), outline stroke 3px deep navy, fill: light gray `#E0E0E0` (muted/lesser)
- Hard offset shadow 5px no blur `#1A1A2E`
- Label at top of tag: small pill "TOOLS LAIN" in dark gray bold caps
- Big price: **"Rp 200rb+"** in very large bold deep navy font, below it "/bulan" in smaller text
- Below the price, 3 small bullet rows with ✗ icons (red X):
  - ✗ Bayar tiap bulan
  - ✗ Kredit expired
  - ✗ Butuh kartu USD
- The whole card is slightly desaturated/gray feeling

**RIGHT CARD — SellOn tag** (slightly tilted +5°, LARGER and more prominent — the hero):
- Shape: same hanging price tag shape, outline stroke 4px deep navy `#1A1A2E`, fill: orange `#FF8A50`
- Hard offset shadow 6px no blur `#1A1A2E`
- Label at top of tag: small pill "SellOn" with flame icon, white text on deep navy background
- Big price: **"Rp 25.000"** in very large bold white font, below it "sekali bayar" in smaller white text
- Below the price, 3 small bullet rows with ✓ icons (white checkmark on purple `#6C5CE7` small circle):
  - ✓ Bayar kalau butuh aja
  - ✓ Kredit nggak expired
  - ✓ QRIS · GoPay · OVO · DANA
- Bottom of this card: small yellow `#FFE156` starburst badge — "MULAI GRATIS" in deep navy bold caps, rotated +8°

**BETWEEN THE TWO CARDS**: a chunky "VS" text badge in deep navy bold, inside a white rounded rectangle with deep navy outline and hard offset shadow — centered between the cards.

**BOTTOM of canvas**:
- Small purple `#6C5CE7` rounded pill: "No subscription. Ever." in white bold caps
- "Slap. Clip. Boom." in deep navy small centered Fredoka bold
- "SellOn" flame brand mark, top-right
