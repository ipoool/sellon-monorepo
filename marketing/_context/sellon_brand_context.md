# SellOn — Brand Context

Use this file to understand the business, audience, and market position before producing any marketing output.

---

## What SellOn Does

- **Product**: SellOn — a WhatsApp-commerce platform for Indonesian SMEs (UMKM)
- **Tagline**: Your online store, straight from WhatsApp
- **Core job**: Helps UMKM sellers open an online storefront at `/{slug}` that accepts orders independently from buyers — without having to reply to chats one by one
- **How it works**:
  1. Seller signs up and creates a store on SellOn (store name, logo, product categories)
  2. Seller adds products to the online catalog
  3. Buyer opens the store link, browses products, and checks out independently (no need to chat the seller first)
  4. Seller receives an order notification, confirms, and ships
  5. Payment goes directly to the seller's Midtrans/bank account — SellOn never holds the money
- **Core promise**: More organized orders, tracked inventory, buyers can purchase anytime — without you having to be on call 24/7.
- **Access model**: Monthly subscription (Free / Pro / Business). The platform does not take a commission on transactions — all sales revenue goes directly to the seller.

---

## Stage & Background

- **Stage**: Indonesian startup, early growth phase
- **Origin**: Built for the Indonesian SME market
- **Market focus**: Indonesia — from Sabang to Merauke, addressing the deeply ingrained habit of ordering via WhatsApp
- **Payment infrastructure**: Midtrans (QRIS, virtual account, GoPay, ShopeePay, credit card) + manual bank transfer + static QRIS — all Midtrans accounts belong to the seller
- **Shipping**: Real-time RajaOngkir integration (JNE, TIKI, POS) + built-in estimates (J&T, SiCepat, AnterAja, GoSend, GrabExpress)
- **Language**: Bahasa Indonesia (primary UI and communications)

---

## Key Differentiators

- **Zero commission** — SellOn charges a flat subscription, not a percentage of each sale. Sellers receive 100% of the transaction value directly to their account.
- **Seller's own Midtrans account** — Buyers pay directly to the seller, not to the platform. No risk of funds being held by a third party.
- **WA-native on the operational side** — Sellers still use WhatsApp for communication, but orders are managed in a structured dashboard instead of a chaotic chat thread.
- **A truly functional online store** — Not just a catalog link. Includes a shopping cart, checkout flow, order page, and automated confirmations.
- **Real-time shipping rates** — Buyers see actual rates from the seller's chosen couriers, not wild guesses.
- **No coding, no hassle** — Store setup in minutes. No technical skills required.
- **Centralized order management** — All orders from all buyers in one place, filterable, exportable, and status-updatable.

## Ideal Customer & Audience

### Primary Audience — Indonesian UMKM Sellers

- **Who**: Small-to-medium online business owners — Instagram shops, resellers, home-based businesses, modern warung owners, UMKM producers who sell directly
- **Age**: 20–45 years old
- **Location**: All across Indonesia, especially tier 1–3 cities
- **Current situation**:
  - Receive orders via personal WA chats or groups
  - Track inventory in a notebook, spreadsheet, or not at all
  - Often overwhelmed replying to chats during busy periods
  - No clear sales records
  - Buyers frequently "ghost" after asking about products
- **Main pain points**:
  - WA orders are chaotic and frequently missed
  - Inventory not tracked — often selling items already out of stock
  - Buyers forget to pay and must be chased manually one by one
  - No customer data for follow-up
  - Looks unprofessional to new buyers
- **Goals**:
  - Accept orders without being on call 24/7
  - Business looks more professional
  - Clean, organized sales records
  - Focus on production/selling, not administration
- **Tech behavior**: Comfortable with smartphones and WhatsApp. Can use marketplaces (Tokopedia, Shopee) but don't want to pay commissions or depend on another platform's algorithm.
- **Buying behavior**: Responds to free trials. Skeptical of big promises. Trusts after seeing real proof (peer seller testimonials, live demos).
- **Key message**: "Open your own online store. Accept orders without the chaos."

### Secondary Audience — Resellers and Dropshippers Leveling Up

- **Who**: Active resellers selling via WA/Instagram who are starting to be overwhelmed by order volume
- **Situation**: Already have returning customers but still managing everything manually. Starting to lose orders because the system isn't organized.
- **Pain**: Can't scale up because operations are still chaotic. No professional order system.
- **Goal**: Have a "store" separate from personal chat, accessible to buyers at any time.
- **Key message**: "Time to level up. Don't let a chaotic chat thread hold your business back."

## Positioning Statement

SellOn is an online store platform built specifically for Indonesian UMKM sellers who currently take orders via WhatsApp. We provide a professional storefront, organized order management, and payment integration directly into the seller's account — zero commission, zero technical complexity.
